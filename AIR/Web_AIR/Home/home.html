AIR GROUP

The Applied Informatics Research (AIR) Group
brings together researchers from within the School
of Design, Communication and IT with collaborators from
across the University of Newcastle and external institutions.

AIR GROUP

Together, the members of the group seek to provide
a collaborative environment to support and further
the diverse research interests of the group
under the umbrella of applied informatics.
