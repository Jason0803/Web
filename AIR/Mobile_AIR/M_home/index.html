Applied Informatics Research Group

The Applied Informatics Research (AIR) Group brings together researchers from within the School of Design, Communication and IT with collaborators from across the University of Newcastle and external institutions. Together, the members of the group seek to provide a collaborative environment to support and further the diverse research interests of the group under the umbrella of applied informatics.
